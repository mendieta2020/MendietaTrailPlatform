## 2. Description of the Service

Quantoryn is a scientific coaching operating system for endurance sports organisations.
It allows coaches to:

- Create and assign structured training plans to athletes.
- Connect athlete accounts to third-party activity providers (e.g. Strava) via OAuth.
- Receive completed activity data from those providers.
- Apply evidence-based analytics (training load, PMC, injury risk) to the resulting data.
- Review Plan vs Real deviations and adapt future training plans accordingly.

The Service is **not** a social network, a public activity sharing platform, or a consumer
fitness application.
